Functionality Notice

This tool is a placeholder. Compressing PDF files (which involves optimizing images, fonts, and internal structures) entirely on the client-side without specialized libraries (like PDF.js for parsing and a custom optimizer, or a WebAssembly port of a PDF tool) is extremely complex.

A production version would typically use server-side processing with tools like Ghostscript or commercial SDKs.
